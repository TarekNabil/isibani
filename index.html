Matric School

Matric is an exciting time of your life. Let’s face it, it’s the last year at school and you and your friends are making it memorable by attending social gatherings, having fun, doing extra-curricular activities, all while trying to balance your studies . With so much going on, it’s very easy to get overwhelmed with all the choices you have to make regarding your future. After all, this is the most important year in your life and a sound matric pass is your ticket to a good tertiary education and landing the job of your dreams. ISIBANI ACADEMY offers Matric School as a strong support structure to your existing school subjects. With tuition in Grades 10, 11 and 12 subjects along with highly qualified educators to help you achieve excellence, your future is off to a rocking start. Classes are offered on a full-time.
